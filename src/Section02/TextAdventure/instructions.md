# Instructions

1. The player is in a forest. They can go either to the east or west.
    1. If east, they fall off a cliff and die.
    2. If west, they find a castle
2. The player is in front of the castle. They can enter either through the door or through the window.
    1. If door, the guard arrests them and lose
    2. If window, they enter
3. The player is in a corridor of the castle. There is a door on the left and right.
    1. If left, they run into the guards and lose
    2. If right, they found the princess and save her
